This topic helps you get started with using the Self Service portal as a Self Service User. Use the portal to create and track incidents and service requests, and view company-wide announcements, FAQs and knowledge articles.

<hint type="info">
  A mobile version of the portal is available for Self Service Mobile users who use smart phones or tablets. For more information, see [Self Service Mobile Portal](self-service-mobile-portal.mdx).
</hint>

## Logging in to the Self Service portal

You must be assigned the Self Service User role to log in to the portal.

1. In a browser, enter the login URL provided by your administrator.
2. Enter your username and password in the login page.
3. Click **Login**. A list of roles displays.
4. Scroll down and select the **Self Service User** role, then click **Submit**.

## Changing your password, language, and profile picture

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To change your password

  1. Log in to the Self Service portal.
  2. Click your username. From the drop-down list, click **Change Password**.

  <Image src="../Resources/images/locale.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../Resources/images/locale.png" githubPath="self/Content/Resources/images/locale.png"></Image>

  3. In the **Change Password** dialog, enter your old password, then enter the new password and confirm it. Note that passwords are case sensitive.
  4. Click **Change**.
  5. Log out and log in again with the new password.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To reset your password

  This feature is only available for internal authorization and does not work for external login.

  1. Click Forgot password? from the main login window.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    *Forgot Password*
  </Paragraph>

  <Image src="../Resources/images/Forgot-Password.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../Resources/images/Forgot-Password.png" githubPath="self/Content/Resources/images/Forgot-Password.png" width="706" height="580" darkWidth="706" darkHeight="580"></Image>

  2. Enter your user name or email address and click **Submit**.

  You will receive a one-time reset link to your registered email address. The link will expire in 20 minutes or as configured by your administrator.

  3. Click the link. You will be re-directed to the **Reset Password** page.
  4. Enter your **User ID**, **New Password** and **Confirm Password**. If you do not know your **User ID**, contact your organization's administrator.
  5. Click **Submit**.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To change the language

  The default language for the application is English (United States). However, if your administrator has given you privileges, you can change the language displayed in the interface to that of your preference. Your changes will be retained, and you will be logged in with the set language until you change it again.

  1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-for-ITSM.}}, click your username from the top menu.
  2. Hover mouse over **Change Locale**. A list of languages set up by your administrator is displayed.

  <Image src="../Resources/images/locale.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="2" initialPath="../Resources/images/locale.png" githubPath="self/Content/Resources/images/locale.png" width="228" height="116" darkWidth="228" darkHeight="116"></Image>

  3. Select the language to use. The application automatically refreshes with the changed locale.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To change your user account profile picture

  1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-for-ITSM.}}, click your username from the top menu.
  2. Click **Change Picture**.
  3. In the **Change Photo** dialog, click **Browse** to select an image file.
  4. Click **Save**. The selected picture is displayed next to your user name.
</ExpandableHeading>
